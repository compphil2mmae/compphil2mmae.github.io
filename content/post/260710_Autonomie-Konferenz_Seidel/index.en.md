The emergence of LLM-based AI systems (LAS) presents several challenges. At the conference "*Autonomy and its Challenges*" {{% mention christian.seidel %}} presents his paper, which concentrates on a comparatively under-explored one: how LAS challenge our understanding of personal epistemic autonomy, i.e., what it means to have beliefs that are genuinely one's own.

More about the event and the paper can be found [here]({{< relref "/event/260710_V_Seidel_Freiburg" >}}).
