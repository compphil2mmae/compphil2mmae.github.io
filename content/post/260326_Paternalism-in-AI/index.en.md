At the high-profile workshop [AI as a Colleague? – Towards a Social Epistemology of AI](https://s8by70av.scispace.co/index.html), held at the University of Bern on 26 and 27 March, {{% mention alina.jacobs %}} will give a talk on epistemic paternalism as part of the [DFG Research-Project on state sass surveillance]({{% ref "/research/esms/" %}}). 

The workshop is organized by [Andreas Wolkenstein](mailto:andreas.wolkenstein@med.uni-muenchen.de) (LMU Munich) and [Claus Beisbart](mailto:claus.beisbart@unibe.ch) (University of Bern) and funded by the Swiss National Science Foundation</br>
{{% mention "alina.jacobs" %}}' talk takes place on [March 26th at 12:00 pm]({{% ref "/event/260326_v_jacobs_ai-colleague/" %}}).
