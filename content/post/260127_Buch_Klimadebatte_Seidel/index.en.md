How can we discuss the future in a better way – in a more differentiated, argument-oriented and level-headed manner?
In their joint [book](https://www.hanser-literaturverlage.de/buch/besser-um-die-zukunft-streiten-9783446285637-t-5875), Barbara Bleisch, Kirsten Meyer, Stefan Riedener, Dominic Roser and {{% mention christian.seidel %}} take a philosophical look at twenty common statements on the climate debate.

The publication will be accompanied by related events (readings, discussions, vernissage). You can find all further information on the [publisher's website](https://www.hanser-literaturverlage.de/buch/besser-um-die-zukunft-streiten-9783446285637-t-5875).
